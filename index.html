Chairman's Letter

The Ross Living Trust Fund was established upon the principles of stewardship, responsibility, continuity, and legacy. Its purpose is to preserve trust assets, maintain orderly administration, and support the long-term vision entrusted to future generations.


True legacy extends beyond financial resources alone. It includes knowledge, values, history, responsibility, and opportunity passed forward with integrity.

Trust Purpose

The Trust exists to provide a framework for preservation, organization, and continuity while maintaining fidelity to its governing instruments and long-term objectives.


Through disciplined stewardship and thoughtful planning, the Trust seeks to protect and preserve resources for present and future beneficiaries.

Legacy

"We inherit more than assets. We inherit responsibility. Legacy is the bridge between what has been entrusted to us and what we leave behind."
